Sample queries

Fetch the first 50 terms

    SELECT * WHERE {
        ?t a coreon:Term .
        ?t coreon:value ?val .
    } LIMIT 50

Fetch 10 terms in French

    SELECT ?t, ?val WHERE {
        ?t a coreon:Term .
        ?t coreon:value ?val .
        ?t coreon:language <http://uri.of/lang#fr> .
    } LIMIT 10
